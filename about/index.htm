    Intro to the LSS

    The Law Students' Society, University of Ibadan is the student body or the assembly of law students at the Faculty of Law, University of Ibadan. This implies that every law student in the University of Ibadan, either freshman, stalites or finalist, belong to this body. The primary aim of the LSS is to ensure the welfare of students and to develop well rounded Law students, excellent academically, socially, and morally that will stand out as distinguished members of the society at large. In order to do this we engage in various academic, administrative, social and moral activities.
