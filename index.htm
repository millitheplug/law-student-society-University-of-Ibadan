LSS Constitution

LAW STUDENT SOCIETY, FACULTY OF LAW, UNIVERSITY OF IBADAN

WELCOME

The Law Students' Society, University of Ibadan is the student body or the assembly of law students at the Faculty of Law, University of Ibadan.
The primary aim of the LSS is to ensure the welfare of students and to develop well rounded Law students, excellent academically, socially, and morally that will stand out as distinguished members of the society at large.
